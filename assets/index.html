Scoring

  • One point per hand

  • Back bonuses: str8=2, flush=4, boat=6, quads=10, str8 flush=15, royal=30
  • Mid bonuses: 2x back bonus
  • Front bonuses: 66=1 pt, 77=2 pt, ... AA=9pt, 222=10pt, AAA=19pt
